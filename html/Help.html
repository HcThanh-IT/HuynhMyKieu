HELP
  • Guidelines for Ordering Ice Cream

    At Happy Ice-cream, the process of buying ice cream is simple and convenient. Customers only need to choose the desired flavor, then they can add toppings and accessories if they wish. After payment at the checkout counter, customers receive the ice cream and can enjoy it right in the store or take it home. Throughout the shopping process, Our staff is always ready to assist and advise to ensure that customers have the easiest and most comfortable shopping experience.

  • Quality of Service from Staff

    Happy Ice-cream is committed to providing high-quality customer service and attentiveness from its staff. We train our staff to always prioritize the comfort and satisfaction of customers. If any issues arise during the service process, we are always willing to listen and address any feedback from customers promptly and professionally. This policy ensures that all customers are served with maximum respect and care.

  • Shipping and Delivery Policy

    Happy Ice-cream focuses solely on providing in-store ice cream service. Currently, we do not offer shipping or delivery services for ice cream orders. We prioritize creating a special and enjoyable ice cream experience for customers when they visit our store. If you want to enjoy our delicious fresh ice cream, please come directly to the store, and we would be delighted to serve you!
